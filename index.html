What is DeLo?

DeLo is a decentralized loyalty platform that allows users to convert traditional loyalty points into blockchain-based tokens. By leveraging the power of decentralized finance (DeFi), users can trade, stake, and manage their loyalty tokens seamlessly, unlocking liquidity and value like never before.
